FAQ

Got questions?
We've got answers.

Does Drafi access and store my emails? +
Drafi uses read-only OAuth permissions: it accesses your emails to surface what matters, but never stores the email content itself. Only the short AI-generated summary is processed. Your inbox data never touches our servers permanently.
What time does the daily update arrive in Slack? +
By default, your daily update arrives at 9:00 AM in your local time zone. You can customize the delivery time during setup, whether that's 7am before you leave home or 8:30am as you sit down at your desk.
Which emails get included in the daily update? +
You decide. In auto-sync mode, Drafi surfaces everything from the last 24 hours and filters out newsletters and promotions automatically. In curated mode, you select specific senders, labels, or keywords; only those appear in Slack. You can switch modes anytime.
How does attachment and link detection work? +
For every item in your daily update, Drafi detects attached files (images, PDFs, spreadsheets) and shared links (Google Drive, WeTransfer, Dropbox, etc.). These appear as clickable cards in your Slack message, so you can open the original file directly from Slack.
What happens after the free 3-month period? +
Drafi will be priced at approximately $35/month after launch. We'll give early access members at least 30 days' notice before any billing starts, and you can cancel at any time. No surprise charges.
